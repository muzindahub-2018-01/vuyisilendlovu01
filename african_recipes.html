Ulusu Lwenkomo (Stewed Ox Tripe)

One of my favourite foods is tripe. Below is a recipe for preparing African style tripe.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg cleaned beef tripe(Ulusu)
  • 1 kg intestines (amathumbu)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • Ground black pepper

Method:

  1. Rinse and ensure the tripe is clean and cut it up into pieces.
  2. Place all the ingredients into a pot and add water until the tripe is completely covered.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat and allow the pot to simmer for about 3 hours.
  4. Season with salt towards the end of the cooking process
